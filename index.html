Pages of Our Stories: Book Club Gathering

Step into the warm and cozy world of words and creativity. "pages of Our Stories", invites book enthusiasts together to connect, share and embrace the magic of words. let's turn the pages together.


Event Details

March 25, 2025, at 6:00 PM

George Brown College, Casa Loma Campus Toronto, ON, Canada

Purpose

The purpose of this gathering is to bring book enthusiasts together to share their love for literature, discuss thought-provoking stories, and inspire connections through words.

Guests & Speakers

Zahra Nazari

Designation: Activist, Author

Zahra Nazari is the passionate organizer of the (Page of our Stories), where readers and thinkers come together to share their love for reading books. Beside managing these kind of events, she herself is an author and public speaker who inspires others with her ability to weave stories that resonate and spark meaningful conversations. Her profound dedication to building a dynamic and thriving community of book enthusiasts is paralleled only by her passion for inspiring and uplifting others through her eloquent speeches and compelling written works.

Edmond Dantès

Designation: Author and Public Speaker

Edmond Dantès is a well-known author whose books have captivated readers around the world, with each release quickly selling out and establishing her name as a leading figure in the literary world. Beyond the pages of her popular novels, Edmond Dantès is an inspiring motivational speaker, known for her ability to connect with audiences and ignite their inner drive to succeed. Her words, whether written or spoken, create a lasting impression that stays with all who come across her speeches.

loa Muerteos

Designation: Literature Professor

loa Muerteos is a literature professor with over 5 years of experience. Known for her engaging discussions, she specializes in analyzing classic and contemporary works. At this book club event, she will lead a lively conversation on her two well-known books, exploring its ideas and significance.
